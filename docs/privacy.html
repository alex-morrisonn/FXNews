Privacy Policy

FXNews is designed to run without an account. It stores your settings on device, uses local notifications if you enable them, and fetches calendar data so the app can show upcoming economic events and trading-session reminders.

No account required On-device settings Local notifications

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Storage

What FXNews Stores

FXNews stores settings such as watched pairs, impact filters, time format, appearance, selected time zone, onboarding status, and notification preferences on your device. FXNews does not require account creation or profile information to use the app.

Alerts

Notifications

If you grant permission, FXNews uses local notifications on your device for economic-event reminders and session or overlap reminders. Notification timing, sound preference, and quiet hours are stored locally and can be changed at any time in the app or in iOS Settings.

Data

Calendar Data

FXNews fetches calendar data from a remote feed and may cache that data locally to improve load time and reliability. Cached data can be cleared from the app's settings screen.

Collection

What FXNews Does Not Collect

FXNews is not designed to collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location, contacts, photos, or payment details through the app.

Tracking

Tracking

FXNews does not use third-party advertising SDKs and is not intended to track you across apps or websites.

Sharing

Sharing

FXNews does not sell your personal information and is not designed to share personal data with advertising networks, data brokers, or third-party analytics providers.

Retention

Retention and Deletion

Preferences and cached calendar data remain on your device until you clear them in the app where available, reset relevant settings, or delete the app from your device.

Contact

Questions

If you have privacy-related questions, or want help clearing information stored by the app on your device, contact feedback@fxnews.app.
