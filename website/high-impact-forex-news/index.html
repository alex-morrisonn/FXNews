High Impact News

Know which forex news events deserve attention.

FXNews highlights major scheduled releases and connects them to the currencies and pairs most exposed to the event.

Inflation

CPI and PCE

Inflation releases can shift interest rate expectations quickly, especially for USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, and NZD pairs.

Jobs

NFP and employment data

Employment reports can drive sharp moves when the numbers change expectations for central bank policy.

Rates

Central bank decisions

Rate statements, press conferences, and policy guidance can affect both immediate volatility and the broader trend.

Growth

GDP, retail sales, and PMI

Growth indicators help traders understand whether an economy is strengthening, slowing, or surprising against forecasts.

Focus on the event, the pair, and the session

High-impact news matters most when it lines up with a liquid trading window and a pair with direct currency exposure. A USD inflation release during the New York session can affect EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, and other USD majors differently depending on positioning and expectations.

FXNews is designed to make those relationships easier to scan before the event starts. It is a planning tool, not a prediction engine.
