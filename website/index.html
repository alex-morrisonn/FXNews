    What does FXNews track?

    FXNews tracks high-impact forex calendar events, affected currency pairs, major market sessions, session overlaps, and iPhone reminders for traders.

    Is FXNews financial advice?

    No. FXNews is an informational forex calendar and market session tool. It does not provide financial advice, broker services, trade signals, or trading recommendations.

    Which pairs can I monitor?

    FXNews highlights majors including EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, AUD/USD, USD/CAD, USD/CHF, and NZD/USD when scheduled news is expected to affect them.
