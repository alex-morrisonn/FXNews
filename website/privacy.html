Privacy Policy

FXNews is built to be useful without asking for an account or unnecessary personal information.

Last updated: June 2026

What the app stores

FXNews may store your app preferences on your device, such as watched pairs, filters, time zone settings, notification settings, and cached calendar data.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, FXNews uses them for event and session reminders. You can change this anytime in the app or iOS Settings.

What we do not collect

FXNews does not require your name, phone number, precise location, contacts, brokerage login, or trading account details.

Website and purchases

The website may receive basic server information needed to load pages. Any App Store purchases are handled by Apple, not directly by FXNews.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to fxnews.app@gmail.com.
