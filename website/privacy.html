Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Overview

FXNews is an informational forex calendar and market session app. We keep data collection limited to what is needed to operate the app, provide support, and process optional subscriptions through Apple.

Information We Collect

FXNews may store app preferences on your device, including notification settings, display settings, selected filters, watchlists, saved startup tab choices, and cached calendar data.

If you contact support by email, we receive the information you choose to include in that message, such as your email address, app issue, screenshots, or device details.

Subscriptions and Payments

FXNews Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive or store your full payment card details. Apple may provide subscription status information needed to unlock Pro features in the app.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, FXNews schedules reminders on your device for calendar events and market sessions. You can manage notification permissions in iOS Settings.

Analytics and Third Parties

FXNews does not sell personal information. The app may rely on Apple services such as App Store, StoreKit, and system notification services. Those services are governed by Apple's privacy policies.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact fxnews.app@gmail.com.
